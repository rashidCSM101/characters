Metrics Explanation:
TP (True Positive): Jo detect hue aur sahi hain
FP (False Positive): Jo detect hue lekin galat hain (noise)
FN (False Negative): Jo detect nahi hue lekin hone chahiye the
Precision: Detected mein se kitne sahi the
Recall: Actual mein se kitne detect hue
F1-Score: Precision aur Recall ka balance
Accuracy: Overall kitne sahi detect hue
